**Topic:** Gamified corporate wellness competition platform - an always-on, company-controlled alternative to "Sykle til jobben"

### Context Guidance

Based on research of "Sykle til jobben":
- Established 1973, ~40,000 annual participants
- Limited to 9-week annual campaign
- Basic gamification (3 points/day max)
- App issues (4.4 rating, UX complaints)
- Limited integrations (Strava/Polar only)

**Key Differentiation Opportunities:**
1. Always-on (not seasonal)
2. Company-controlled competitions
3. Advanced gamification
4. Better UX/app quality
5. More integrations

1. **Role Playing:** Embody stakeholders (employees, HR, admins, competitors) to discover hidden needs and friction points

## Session Summary

**Session Completed:** 2026-01-13
**Duration:** ~90 minutes
**Ideas Generated:** 115
**Techniques Used:** Role Playing, Cross-Pollination, Reverse Brainstorming

### Key Insights

1. **"Consistency > Performance"** is the philosophical core - design for behavior change, not athletic competition

2. **Flexibility is the weapon** - year-round, custom competitions, fair systems - everything Sykle til jobben isn't

3. **Lars is the user** - design for the reluctant participant, not the fitness enthusiast

4. **Nina is the buyer** - she needs ROI proof, simple pricing, and a platform that won't embarrass her

5. **Erik is the gatekeeper** - if setup is painful, adoption fails before it starts

### Breakthrough Concepts

- **Activity Enablement** - not just tracking, but helping people DO activities
- **Winner Duels** - playoff structure creates narrative and excitement
- **Company vs Company Leagues** - external motivation + employer branding
- **Smart Timing Nudges** - become a partner, not just a tool
